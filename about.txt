About:

Key Sheet Music Collction,abbreviated as KSMC,is a compilation of music sheets initiated by zxxxxxx(capura),ipushu and 
I(jesse629/solairef) in January of 2009.It has been updated multiple times and includes music sheets of the following products
shown on the picture below(the picture can be found at my blog). Besides the piano score,the ones of the instruments such as 
violin,viola,cello,numbered(simplified),guitar,flute,orchestra,drum are also included.The file formats contained are pdf,mus,jpg,midi,ove,gp5,gp4,ptb,gif,jpg,png,bmp,sdx.
So far,the collection has more than 1300 music sheets and over 2000 midis, it is best for the ones who love the art of Key-Style music. 

 
 

关于:

Key Sheet Music Collection,又名Key系乐谱集,是我(jesse629/solairef),zxxxxxx(capura)与ipushu于09年1月份开始整理,收集的谱子集.

目前已更新十几次,包括了下图中的所有作品.其中谱子不光是钢琴谱,亦有小提琴谱,中提琴谱,大提琴谱,吉他谱,简谱,长笛谱,团奏谱,鼓谱等等.格式多样,

包括了pdf,mus,jpg,midi,ove,gp5,gp4,ptb,gif,jpg,png,bmp,sdx等等.到目前为止谱集已经收录了超过1300张谱子和2000多个midi,

是喜爱Key系音乐的朋友们绝佳的材料.
